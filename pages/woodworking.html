Woodworking & Furniture Making

Started: 2021

Woodworking has become one of my most fulfilling hobbies. A perfect weekend often means spending several hours in my garage, working on a project. Experimenting with different tools, discovering new techniques is as much fun as the finished project.

I first got into woodworking when I tried to replicate an expensive floor lamp, and built a similar one using just a handsaw. The result was a wobbly and ugly lamp.
